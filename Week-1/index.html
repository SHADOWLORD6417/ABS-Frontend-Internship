About Us

Hotel Details

An upscale experience awaits you in the heart of Mumbai. Located in close proximity to major landmarks of the city; a short walk from key tourist attractions, shopping destinations and theatres.

Our hotel in Mumbai is located just 6.5 kilometers from the Mumbai Central railway station, 7.5 kilometers from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus and 33 kilometers from the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport, Mumbai.

From the trendy texture of the all-wooden exterior to polished marble and granite, glass panels, ABS Mumbai is not only uber cool but also a classic. Delight your taste buds to the rich and diverse dining experiences, offering the best from the world. Our 5 star hotel in Mumbai has everything that a luxury traveler can imagine! Join us to feel the pulse of an international business and conferencing destination.
